Project Management & Enterprise Project Management Office (EPMO)

We prioritize strong project management on technology projects. This requires project management staff who understand what the end-user needs, how to effectively triage issues for resolution, and what to raise to government counterparts.

We also have engagements where we serve in an Enterprise Project Management Office (EPMO) capacity. EPMO is a staff extender model that allows the government to have a vendor manage the project schedule, manage other vendors, and prioritize needs. Read more about how EPMO fits well with agile development practices.
